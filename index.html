Upload

  • Upload the image of the cast in which you wish to place the BLP.
  • Make sure to photograph the cast in a plane perpendicular to the camera with the index BLP placed close to the cut surface of the cast.
  • Do not worry about the dimensions of the photograph. You will be able to calibrate the BLP to match the size of the cast in the next step.

Calibrate

  • In order to calibrate your image to the BLP accurately, place the BLP on the screen over the BLP in your picture.
  • Resize by clicking and dragging the squares around the picture.
  • Rotate by clicking and dragging on the circle next to the image.
  • Adjust the image such that the margins of both the BLPs coincide.
  • Now the BLP is proportionate to the resolution of your cast picture. Proceed to the next step to place the BLP.
You can also use the arrow keys on the keyboard to move the BLP. Use Shift + arrow keys to move the blp faster.

Position

  • Rotate and move the BLP to place it anywhere you wish on the surface of the cast by clicking and dragging the BLP image.
  • When you are done, proceed to the next step to save the image.
The black mark on the BLP indicate the shoulder of the implant. The red marks are 3mm above the shoulder of the implant.

Save

Right click on the image that has opened in the new window/tab to save it in a format that will allow you to print and email the image.
